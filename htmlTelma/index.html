The beginning is filled with chaos.
From this chaos, a universe is made, and you have a part in this creation.
Your choices will change what it becomes … and what it becomes, may change you.

Take control of a special particle in a brand new universe. Navigate your way through various levels while dogding hazards such as black holes or harmful particles and take advantage of your special attributes to use the other particles around you to your own goals.
